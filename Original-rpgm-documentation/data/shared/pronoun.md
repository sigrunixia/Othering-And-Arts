# pronoun

The pronoun is a key that identifies the pronoun to be used for a [player](../../components/character.md) or a
[non-player](../../components/non-player-character.md) character.


Accepted Values:
- they
- she
- he
- it
- ae
- e
- per
- ve
- xe
- ze
